### **Part 1: Standard Documents (Prepare Now)**
These are academic, identity, and payment records that you already have or can obtain independently of the institute-specific registration forms.

* [ ] **Class X Board Certificate and Mark Sheet** (as proof of Date of Birth).
* [ ] **Class XII Mark Sheets and Passing Certificate**.
* [ ] **JEE (Advanced) 2025 Admit Card**.
* [ ] **JoSAA Provisional Offer and Seat Acceptance Letter** / Branch & Institute Allotment Letter.
* [ ] **Medical Fitness Certificate** (the copy submitted during the JoSAA portal registration).
* [ ] **Copy of Aadhaar Card** (or another government-issued address proof).
* [ ] **Passport-size Photographs** (carry at least 4 to 6 copies).
* [ ] **Proof of Seat Acceptance Fee Payment** made to JoSAA/JEE.
* [ ] **Proof of Remaining Fee Payment** (full fee payment) made to the respective IIT.

### **Part 2: Institute-Specific Forms (To be completed when officially released)**
These forms are released by the respective IITs closer to the physical reporting date. You will need to download, print, fill, and sign them once they are made available.

#### **Registration & Administrative Forms**
* [ ] **Registration Form - Academics Section (Form A)**
* [ ] **Registration Form - Student Affairs Section (Form A1)**
* [ ] **Joining Report cum Undertaking** (IIT Mandi specific)
* [ ] **Hindi Name Form** (for your degree certificate spelling)

#### **Campus Policies & Agreements**
* [ ] **The Honour Code (Form E)**: Student commitment regarding academic integrity.
* [ ] **IT Policy Guidelines (Form I)**: Computing resources acceptable usage policy agreement.
* [ ] **Academic Rules Awareness Undertaking (Form C)**
* [ ] **Vaccination Self-Declaration (Form C)**: Vaccination history for Typhoid, MMR, Hepatitis A & B, Chickenpox, etc.

#### **Hostel & Welfare Forms**
* [ ] **Medical Facilities Awareness Undertaking (Form B)**
* [ ] **Vehicle Restriction Undertaking (Form B)**: Mandatory for hostel residents, agreeing not to keep motor vehicles on campus.
* [ ] **Insurance Scheme Nomination Form (Form F)**: Mandatory nomination form requiring your signature and signatures of two witnesses.
* [ ] **Benevolent Fund Scheme Nomination Form (Form G)**

#### **Anti-Ragging Undertakings**
* [ ] **Online Anti-Ragging Reference Number**: Obtained by registering at [www.antiragging.in](http://www.antiragging.in).
* [ ] **UGC Anti-Ragging Undertaking (Form H)**: To be signed by both you and your parent/guardian.
* [ ] **Supreme Court Anti-Ragging Undertaking (Form H-1 / Mandi equivalent)**: Endorsed by you and your parent/guardian.
